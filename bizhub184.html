Bizhub 184/164

HomeAboutBizhub 184/164

content

Its compact dimensions allow it to be flexibly located in any work space, providing both strong functionality and good looks.


Standard cassette holds 500 sheets of A4 paper (64g/m2 - 157g/m2). Easy-to-use guide for changing paper size. The large paper-feed roller not only increases paper-feed reliability, it also reduces paper-tray vibration and operation noise when printing.


The optional Multi Bypass tray can accommodate up to 100 sheets of paper, from A5 all the way up to A3 size. A wide range of paper weights can be used, from 64g/m2 to 157g/m2.

benefits

Low power consumption

The bizhub 184/164 has been designed to minimise environmental impact with a specification that surpasses the international ENERGY STAR Program Version.

Compact & lightweight design

A compact, lightweight design (weight reduced by approx. 42%, number of parts reduced by approx. 35%) means fewer raw materials consumed and a reduction in the amount of CO2 emitted during manufacture.

Polymerised toner for superior image quality

Compared to conventional toner,Konica Minolta's Simitri HD polymerised toner uses smaller, more uniform particles. Small text is sharp, while gradations and solid black are beautifully reproduced.

Clear & simple operation

The simplified operation panel with its familiar cursor key configuration and navigation makes the bizhub 184/164 extremely intuitive to use.
