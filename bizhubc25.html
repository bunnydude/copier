Bizhub C25

HomeAboutBizhub C25

content

When every minute counts, the bizhub C25 puts a fast, high quality document solution close at hand. Designed to fit anywhere, this compact color printer features fast, brilliant color print/copy quality to improve your image, built-in print control and fax to boost your output and an energy-saving performance to lower your cost.


25 ppm print/copy output in color and B&W to keep ahead of your workflow. Compact design with bold black styling for center-office placement. Fast first-copy output time helps make you more productive. 4.3inch color touch-panel display makes operation simple even for first-time users.


Built-in Emperon print system with PCK6 and PS3 emulation for cross-platform productivity. 850-sheet maximum paper capacity in weights up to 110 lb index, standard duplexing of heavy stock. High-yield Simitri HD toner with biomass reduces the environmental impact of the compact color printer. Built-in Super G3 fax transmission/reception for all-in-one productivity.
