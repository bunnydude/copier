Bizhub C35

HomeAboutBizhub C35

content

The bizhub C35 may be the smallest color laser printer in its class with big advantages for your document workflow, including high-speed 31ppm color output, superior Simitri HD high-resolution images, built-in Emperon print control and standard auto duplex.


31 ppm print output in color and B&W to keep up with peak document demands. Ultra-compact design: just 16.5inch wide and 13inch high at a weight of approx. 60 lbs. 4.3inch color touch-panel display makes operation simple even for first-time users or temporary office assistants. Built-in Emperon print system with universal printer driver, Postscript 3, PCL 6 and native XPS emulations.


High-yield Simitri HD toner with biomass to reduce environmental impact. Simple connectivity with Gigabit Ethernet, USB 2.0 and USB 1.1 interface. 850-sheet maximum paper capacity in weights up to 216 gsm, with standard auto duplexing. Built-in Super G3 fax transmission/reception for all-in-one productivity.

Key Facts

The Konica Minolta bizhub C35 is a Color copy machine. This copier has a maximum recommended monthly duty cycle of 120,000 pages, which is 20% more than the average for all Copiers. The Konica Minolta bizhub C35 copier supports the following standard interfaces: Ethernet, USB and USB 2.0.

Paper Handling

The Konica Minolta bizhub C35's paper tray has a maximum capacity of 100 sheets, which is the average for all Copiers. The Konica Minolta bizhub C35 also includes a paper casette that will store up to 250 sheets, which is significantly less than the average for all Copiers. The Konica Minolta bizhub C35 has an Auto Document Feeder that has a maximum capacity of 50 sheets, which is the average for all Copiers.

Copying

The maximum black and white copy speed of the Konica Minolta bizhub C35 is 31 PPM, which is 2 PPM slower than the average for all Copiers. Additionally, the Konica Minolta bizhub C35 has a first copy out time of 12 seconds, which is 73.9% faster than the average for all Copiers.

Power Consumption

The maximum power consumption of the Konica Minolta bizhub C35 is 1200 watts, which is slightly lower than the average for all Copiers.
