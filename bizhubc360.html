Bizhub C360/C280/C220

HomeAboutBizhub C360/C280/C220

content

New bizhub colour C360/C280/C220 MFPs with refined black-and-white-line design maximise document solutions and network security. New inner finisher, engine and advanced processing for superb image quality, plus enhanced UI and major functions enable higher productivity and reliability for TCO reduction.


Improved image quality through enhanced measures to prevent striped copies. Improved FCOT and FPOT Short warm-up time (B/W: 30 seconds, colour: 45 seconds) 100% duplexing productivity.


benefits

Improved paper jam removal

Larger workspace and improved visibility of paper path through simpler paper path design and a wide-opening right side cover.

Environmentally friendly design

Low power consumption. Quiet operation (Industry-leading). Resource-friendly design makes active use of recycled materials. Compliance with environmental standards

Improved image quality

Reproduction of minute text and fine lines. Multi-Generation Copies. Colour Reproduction. Enlarged/reduced images. Reproduction of text in halftone dot areas

Security enhancements

Expanded new biometric authentication unit AU-102 and non-contact IC card authentication unit AU-201. IEEE 802.1X support. NDS authentication over TCP/IP. Enhanced unauthorised copy prevention feature (Copy Guard/Password Copy).
