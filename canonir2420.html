Canon IR 2420

HomeAboutCanon IR 2420

content

This space-saving device provides highly efficient black and white copying plus local printing via USB at up to 20ppm/cpm. With optional network printing and scanning, this affordable device is ideal for small offices.


The new space-saving range of copiers provides highly efficient copying plus local printing via USB. They are ideal for busy, small workgroups looking for a productive solution with low cost of ownership.


benefits

Scalable to suit your requirements

These compact models can be configured to cope with big challenges. With a choice of additional paper drawers, you can select the capacity that best suits your needs up to 1,080 sheets, in a variety of paper sizes up to A3.

Lower environmental impact

These ENERGY STAR qualified models offer impressive energy efficiency, as low as 4.5 watts power consumption in sleep mode, to save energy and cost. With Canon On-Demand fixing technology there is rapid recovery from sleep mode, so you can save energy without affecting productivity.

Be more productive

Users can do more, faster, with the simple and intuitive to use backlit 5line LCD. With high quality copies at 20ppm and exceptional responsiveness 7.9 seconds first copy out time the imageRUNNER 2420 saves you time.

Excellent reliability and support

Continuous high quality output and peace of mind are provided with improved durability and serviceability. Canon world class service and support provides expert installation, training and on-site services, plus pre determined Service Level Agreements.
