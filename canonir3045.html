Canon IR 3045

HomeAboutCanon IR 3045

content

The imageRUNNER 3045 offers a full range of document handling options that support the creation of professional-looking documents. There is also support for advanced digital copying, network scanning, and Mail Box features, as well as optional network printing, single or dual-line fax capabilities, and the ability to distribute electronic information.


Operating at a speed of up to 45 ppm (letter), the imageRUNNER 3045 device is an advanced multifunction solution that can be personalized to help mid to large sized workgroups bring streamlined document production capabilities in-house.

benefits

High Quality

Operating at up to 45 pages-per-minute, the imageRUNNER 3045 is equipped with a 20GB Hard Disk Drive and 512MB RAM (upgradeable to 768MB RAM), providing users with exceptional productivity and reliability.

Digital Copies

With a scanning resolution of 600 x 600 dpi, enhanced copy resolution of 1200 x 600 dpi, and enhanced print resolution of 2400 x 600 dpi, the imageRUNNER 3045 delivers superior quality for the most faithful reproduction of text and images.

MEAP Architecture

Incorporating Canon MEAP? (Multifunctional Embedded Application Platform) architecture, the imageRUNNER 3045 devices applications can be customized to meet unique business needs. The customized applications run from the imageRUNNER 3045 control panel streamlining document workflows.

user friendly

The color user interface panel and intuitively designed device drivers provide user-friendly operation for both walk-up and desktop environments.
