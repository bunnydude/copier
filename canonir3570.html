Canon IR 3570

HomeAboutCanon IR 3570

content

By combining document handling efficiency with simplicity, Canon can help you maximize workflow and still minimize cost and effort. To save money, the iR3570 has a modular design and a built-in MEAP Java platform. So it can be set up to match your current needs and easily accommodate future growth and further personalized business applications. To save time, complex functions are achievable in easy steps at the device itself or individual workstations. To optimize your resources, Canon Utilities provide a range of tools, which allow ease of use and control of the devices on your network.
