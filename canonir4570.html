Canon IR 4570

HomeAboutCanon IR 4570

content

The Canon iR 4570 can print, scan, copy, fax, communicate electronically, finish documents, manage workflow, all with document security. It is a flexible, affordable device for medium office workgroups.


Canon iR 4570 B/W multi-functional printer offers enhanced performance for sharing and creating documents in medium office workgroups.


The iR 4570 is simple to install and, to save time setting up and managing several devices, you can use Canon device information distribution to repeat the settings on one device automatically on others. Take advantage of Canon Utility software to manage your Canon devices and control print workflow easily. And maximise uptime through the unrivalled service support offered by Canon built-in e-Maintenance.

benefits

Communicate electronically and process speedily

Use Universal Send, to scan in documents and send them electronically, thus saving time and costs. PDFs can be created at the iR 4570 itself and retrieved using Canon searchable PDF feature.

Create and collate complex documents with ease

Complete all your documents in-house through the devices range of finishing options. For easy document collation, use Canon intelligent iW Publishing Manager software to merge and collate documents electronically from different Windows applications (Microsoft Word, Excel, etc).

Improve your document security

Security innovations on the iR 4570 keep your confidential data well protected. Secure Print delays printing until you are at the device to enter a password. ID Management and Single Sign On features limit access to the device through pre-set passwords.

Easy to use today and future proof for tomorrow

The intuitive control panel on the device and Remote User Interface (RUI) at the user PC, make sophisticated document handling tasks simple. By building in a Java based MEAP platform on our iR devices, we are also giving them the potential to incorporate many new applications currently being developed to help the way you work.
